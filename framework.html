Executive Summary RCSA Register Framework Mapping Scenario Forecasting

RCSA

RCSA is the practical method used by departments to identify risks, assess control effectiveness, and evaluate residual exposure.

COSO

COSO provides the internal control structure through its five components and related principles, making it especially relevant to audit, compliance, finance, and governance teams.

ISO 31000

ISO 31000 provides the broader risk management lens, helping frame identification, analysis, evaluation, treatment, and monitoring across the institution.
